Welcome to Bitrig!

Bitrig is a free, fast, and secure Unix-like Open Source operating system. It is available on current hardware platforms. The source code is freely available under a permissive license.

We are in the process of getting our first public release ready. Please be patient and check back often to see where we are at. The plan is to have something available for public consumption soon. We currently do have snapshots available.

If you are interested in hacking on an OS please come talk to us on IRC. We hang out at #bitrig on irc.mindcry.org.
